About Us

Jaticom Sdn.Bhd

Jaticom is a private computer shop belongs to Mr. Hj. Kamarul which has been operating for several years in business. Jaticom is located in Batu Berendam, Melaka which is very close to Melaka city. Jaticom distributes used & new computers to customers from various industries. For example, factories that produce products such as chipsets and so on at CTRM Composite Technology Research Malaysia, Infineon Technologies Melaka, DOMINANT Opto Technologies and others. Jaticom also sells laptops, printers and various types of computer equipment needed. In addition, it also accepts computer/laptop repairs such as upgrading RAM, SSD or installing the necessary software at reasonable prices.




There are three full-time employees at Jaticom, and they work in one of three roles: Senior Technician, Junior Technician, or Administrator. To this end, Jaticom is committed to growing its presence in online marketing and taking an active role in other forms of marketing.
